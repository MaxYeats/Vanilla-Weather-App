About me

As a curious kid I imagined myself growing up and becoming either a scientist or an artist, but I ended up walking a different professional path. Thanks to some down time (pandemic?), a lot of research and this great coding workshop (SheCodes!) I rediscovered my old passion which is being curious and creating art. Through software development I have been able to create my own art while interacting with the public and engaging in some science. I will be sharing more about me and my work as @maxyeats on Twitter and Instagram.
